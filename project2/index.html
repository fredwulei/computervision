Warping Images

In the process of warping image, I warped point of image to a spherical coordinate. And I also applied radial distortion.

Radial Distortion

During the process of finding correct parameter of radial distortion, I took photos of the checkbox images with the same setting of the camera. Then I imported the images from different angle to "Jean-Yves Bouguets" toolkit package and calculated the value of focus length, k1 and k2.

Computing Features

I used harris corner detection of detecting features from lab1, found all the points of interest in every images, then also from the lab1, I used my simplified SIFT algorithm here to output the description of every features.

Matching Features and Aligning Pairs

In matching features, I used SSD to find the best match of every features. After that, I used RANSAC algorithm to randomly pick initial points and build the model ( here I used translation to model the transform of images), ran this codes for 200 rounds and found the best solution of translation.
